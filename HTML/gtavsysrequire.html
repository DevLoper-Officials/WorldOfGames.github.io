System Requirements For The Game Grand Theft Auto V




PC Version Requirements(Minimum)

RAM: 4 GB Ram
Required Space: 120 GB
OS: Windows (7,10,Upper Versions), Standlone, MAC
CPU: Intel Core i5
CPU Speed: 4.00GHz
VIDEO CARD: Nvidia 667 RTX/AMD Radeon HD 2900 or equivalent 512MB DirectX 9c card.
PIXEL SHADER: 3.0.
VERTEX SHADER: 3.0.
SOUND CARD: Yes.
DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 512 MB. [May Cause Super Unstable FPS And Not Playable For Low End PC Gamers]


PC Version Requirements(Recommended)

RAM: 8 GB Ram
Required Space: 150 GB [Because Of Regular Updates Of GTA Online]
OS: Windows (7,10,Upper Versions), Standlone, MAC
CPU: Intel Core i7
CPU Speed: 5.00GHz
VIDEO CARD: Nvidia Geforce RTX 3080 ti/AMD Radeon 2GB Video Cards And DirectX 11 Comportable card.
PIXEL SHADER: 3.0.
VERTEX SHADER: 3.0.
SOUND CARD: Yes.
DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 2 MB. [Will Give A Super Smooth Gameplay On Ultra High Graphics And Also Streamable]
