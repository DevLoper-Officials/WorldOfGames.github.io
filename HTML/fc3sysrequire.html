System Requirements For The Game Far Cry 3






PC Version Requirements(Minimum)

CPU: Intel Core2 Duo E6700/AMD Athlon64 X2 6000+
CPU SPEED: 2.25Ghz
RAM: 2 GB.
VIDEO CARD: Nvidia 8800 GTX/AMD Radeon HD 2900 or equivalent 512MB DirectX 9c card.
PIXEL SHADER: 3.0.
VERTEX SHADER: 3.0.
SOUND CARD: Yes.
DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 512 MB. [May Cause Unstable FPS But Still Playable For Low End PC Gamers]


PC Version Requirements(Recommended)

CPU: Intel Core i3-530
CPU SPEED: 3.00Ghz
RAM: 4 GB.
VIDEO CARD: Nvidia 3360 RTX/AMD Radeon 7970 or equivalent 1GB DirectX 9c card.
PIXEL SHADER: 3.0.
VERTEX SHADER: 3.0.
SOUND CARD: Yes.
DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 1 GB.
